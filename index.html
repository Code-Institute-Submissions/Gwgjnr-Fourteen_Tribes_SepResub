Why Choose Galway?

Food and Drink

With over 475 pubs and restaurants to pick from, we are certain you will find exactly what you are looking for in the city of tribes. Enjoy a creamy pint of Guinness in rooftop bars overlooking the Altantic and wake to bottomless processo brunches in the heart of the city. From 6 michelin starred restaurants to the burger voted best in Ireland, we have it all.

Festivals

There is a reason Galway is called the Festival capital of Ireland. From the visual arts, live music & drama to horse-racing, Galway plays host to something for everyone. From the foodies, you can check out the Galway Food Festival and over Christmas, Galway's Christmas Market in Eyre Square has become a truly highlight event in recent years drawing people back year on year.

Hotels

From balcony rooms with beautiful views of Salthill promenade to luxurious boutique hotels nestled just off Eyre Square, You will be spoilt for choice when picking your preferred place to stay. With over 200 hotels, suitable for everything from Hen parties to romantic couple getaways, you will always find something to match your needs and pocket.
