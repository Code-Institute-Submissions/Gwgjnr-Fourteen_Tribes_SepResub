Hotels

The House Hotel

Located just around the corner from the ever bustling Quay Street and Shop Street, The House Hotel is Galway’s unique city centre boutique hotel and home to Galway’s favourite cocktail bar, The Yard Kitchen/ Bar, the newest addition to the Galway social scene, The Yard is a favourite amongst our guests and Galway locals alike. Our 4 star hotel in Galway is perfect for anyone visiting the city of the tribes, be it for business, leisure, avail of one of our Galway hotel deals, girls weekend away, a romantic night away to spoil someone special, or to enjoy any of the endless Galway festivals.

Galway Bay Hotel

Galway Bay Hotel is a luxurious 4 star hotel at the heart of the Wild Atlantic Way, located 5 minutes from Galway City. Nestled on the shores of the breathtaking Galway Bay, Galway Bay Hotel offers great accommodation which comprises of 153 bedrooms, fine and casual dining restaurants, 6 purpose built conferencing suites, leisure centre and beauty rooms. When you stay at Galway Bay Hotel, you can choose from shopping, sightseeing, outdoor activities or even opt for Galway's lively nightlife. So whether you want to experience a bit of the culture and arts or relax and unwind, Galway has it all.

The G hotel & Spa

Meticulously designed by world-renowned milliner Phillip Treacy, the G Hotel & Spa is a luxurious, glamorous and inspirational 5-star destination. But above all else, it’s a unique experience. Perched on the edge of Europe along Ireland’s legendary Wild Atlantic Way, you’ll be five minutes from the centre of one of the most vibrant and cosmopolitan cities in the world. However, inside this magical, creative space, you’ll feel like your worlds away from anywhere.
