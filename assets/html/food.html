Food & Drink

Aniar Restaurant

Aniar is a Michelin Starred restaurant nestled in Galway’s West End. All their food ingredients are sourced in the west of Ireland. Their goal is to showcase the various flavours that describe the west of Ireland. Their ever-changing and evolving menu is dictated by the seasons meaning you will never see the same menu twice. The kitchen is lead by chef patron JP McMahon. Alongside using wild and foraged local ingredients, Jp believes in being traditional cooking and preservation methods back to life, such as curing, smoking and fermenting.

An Pucan

An Pucan Galway is probably a must see for any vistors looking to experience an Irish Bar. They are located on the corner of Eyre Square in the beating heart of Galway. With a fantastic chef, great bar staff and beautiful interior, An Pucan is a great place to eat or party, morning, noon and night. With over 200 whiskey’s on offer and all day dining, make sure to pop in and say hi.

John Keogh's Gastro-Bar

Its hard to believe that its only been 4 years since this establishment opened its doors, with multiple national awards and a core local customer base, John Keoghs has quickly becoome one of Ireland’s best-loved gastrobars. Named as one of Irelands best gastropubs by Hospitality Ireland in 2017, but they didnt rest there, with The Irish Independent naming John Keoghs in their Top 20 Gastro Pubs in Ireland. Filled with snugs and partitions with genuine pub antiques sourced from around Ireland. This along with an open fire and award-winning customer service ensures you will never leave disappointed.
