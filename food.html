Food & Drink

Aniar Restaurant

Aniar is a Michelin Star restaurant nestled in Galway’s West End. All their food ingredients are sourced in the west of Ireland. Their goal is to showcase the various flavours that describe the west of Ireland. Their unique and evolving menu is dictated by the seasons meaning you will rarely see the same menu twice. Alongside using wild and foraged local ingredients, they believes in showcasing traditional Irish cooking techniques, such as curing, smoking and fermenting.

An Pucan

An Pucan Galway is located on the corner of Eyre Square in the bustling heart of Galway. This pub is renowned for its incredible events and beautiful decor. With a fantastic chef, great bar staff and beautiful interior, An Pucan is a great place to eat or party, morning, noon and night. With over 200 whiskey’s on offer and all day dining, make sure to pop in and say hi.

John Keogh's Gastro-Bar

Its hard to believe that its only been 4 years since this establishment opened its doors with multiple national awards already under their belt. John Keoghs has quickly become one of Ireland’s best-loved gastrobars. Named as one of Irelands best gastropubs by Hospitality Ireland in 2017, they didnt rest there with The Irish Independent naming John Keoghs in their Top 20 Gastro Pubs in Ireland. You have your pick of snugs and partitions while surrounded by genuine pub antiques sourced from around Ireland. This along with an roaring fire and second to none customer service ensures you will never leave disappointed.
