Hotels

The House Hotel

This 4 star hotel truly is a unique experience with a striking interior. You could not ask for a better location as you will be nestled on a quiet lane just off bustling Quay Street and Shop Street. With a fantastic cocktails menu and delicious menu you may be tempted to stay in. This hotel is suitable for all groups, from romance adventures to groups you can expect a tailored experience.

Galway Bay Hotel

This establishment is a truly comfortable stay located on the Salthill Promenade, overlooking Galway Bay. With 153 bedrooms, 2 restaurant and a beautiful leisure centre you will be spoilt for choice. With easy access to both the city and connemara you can make the best of both worlds and make sure to experience everything Galway has to offer.

The G hotel & Spa

This 5 star hotel was designed in partership with world-renowned milliner Phillip Treacy. The dramatic design is carried throughout the Signature Lounges, Cocktail bar, restaurant, spa and 101 luxurious bedrooms. Your every need will be met before you even notice when you book a stay in the G Hotel. This place is frequented by celebrities, having recently played host to Ed Sheeran while touring.
